About Us!

In 2017 a void was noticed in the central valley; A provider was needed who could cater to the people, no matter the budget, for quality outdoor furniture. Thats when W Furniture opened their doors. The concept is to provide customers with a vast range of innovative and sophisticated choices that would meet specific taste’s and needs. Whether you’re looking for a dining set, sofa sets, bars, chaise lounges, fire pits, all weather game tables or umbrella’s we are dedicated to helping you create a space you will not want to leave!
